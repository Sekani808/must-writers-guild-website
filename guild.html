Who We Are

Our mission, vision, and values

Mission

To nurture creative writing talent at Malawi University of Science and Technology by providing a supportive community where writers can develop their craft, share their work, and inspire others through the power of storytelling.

Vision

To become the premier literary community in Malawi's higher education landscape, producing published authors, award-winning poets, and influential voices that shape our national narrative.

Our Values
