Why Join MUST Writers Guild?

Be part of a vibrant community that celebrates creativity, supports growth, and transforms passion into artistry.

Improve Writing Skills

Access workshops, feedback sessions, and writing challenges designed to hone your craft.

Constructive Feedback

Receive thoughtful critiques from peers and mentors to elevate your work.

Competitions

Participate in local and national writing competitions with guild support.

Connect with Writers

Build lasting friendships with fellow storytellers, poets, and creative minds.

Membership Requirements

  • Must be a current student at Malawi University of Science and Technology
  • Have a genuine interest in creative writing, poetry, or storytelling
  • Commit to attending at least one guild event per semester and be a consistent attendee at guild meetings
  • Be willing to provide constructive feedback to fellow members
  • Submit one original piece for the guild library within the first month
